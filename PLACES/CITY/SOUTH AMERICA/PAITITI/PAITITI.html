PAITITI

Introduction

Incan legends tell of a lost city hidden in the remote rainforests of southeast Peru, northern Bolivia, or northwest Brazil where the hero Inkarri, after founding Q,ero and Cusco lived out the rest of his days in refuge. For the invading Spanish it was a lost city of unimaginable wealth and riches which they spent years searching for, but has it ever been found?

Literary

Paititi represents not only a treasure trove of material riches, but also a symbol of power and mystery. This lost city is believed to hold profound secrets about the Inca civilization, its rituals, wisdom, and achievements.

This city is actually an extension of the ancient and mythological empire of El Dorado and its inhabitants, known as "the Amazons." They were initially a completely matriarchal people who welcomed the planet's refugee priestesses when Marduk ordered their slaughter and enslavement. Under the protection of their masters and federated groups, this culture built its empire on an ancient civilization that existed long before. Patiti is the sum of three distinct civilizations, the oldest of which dates back approximately 4 million years, when the Amazon was in a different geological configuration and most of it was seabed. This city is currently connected to the Temple of the 22 Rays and other extraterrestrial cities in the region, and they are the guardians of the Amazonian pyramids. It is a school for the rescue of priestesses, and most of the telluric thrones of the lebaras contain ascended beings who use this city as a point of interaction with the rest of the temples on Earth. A large portion of the cryogenic bodies of the ancient priestesses are stored in this city, which is under the protection of the Intergalactic Confederation and the Galactic Council. from the book: "Orixá Reiki" Author: Rodrigo Romo
